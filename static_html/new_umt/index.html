Frequently Asked Questions

What services does UMT Global Logistics provide?

UMT Global Logistics specializes in reliable freight transportation services across the United States, including dry van trucking, expedited delivery, and dedicated carrier solutions tailored to meet diverse business needs.

2. Where is UMT Global Logistics located?

Our headquarters is proudly based in 1900 North Aurora Road, Naperville, IL 60563, allowing us to efficiently serve clients throughout the Midwest and nationwide.

Is UMT Global Logistics hiring drivers?

Yes, we are always looking for professional and experienced drivers to join our team. Visit the "Get Started" now section of our website to learn more and apply online.

What makes UMT Global Logistics different from other carriers?

We combine personalized service, real-time tracking, and a commitment to safety and punctuality. Our clients trust us for dependable delivery and strong communication every step of the way.

We provide real-time shipment tracking for all our clients. Once your load is dispatched, you’ll receive tracking details and updates through our logistics platform or directly from our dispatch team.
